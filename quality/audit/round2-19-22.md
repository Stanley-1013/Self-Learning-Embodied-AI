### Ch22 Multimodal LLM Robotics

- **[MED]** 22-multimodal-llm-robotics.md:115 and :123 and :436 — **Open X-Embodiment institution count is wrong in three places**. The OXE paper (Padalkar et al. 2023, arXiv 2310.08864) states explicitly **21 institutions** contributed data, covering **22 different robot embodiments**. Chapter writes "22 機構" in all three references. This is a factual error — the "22" refers to robot types, not institutions. — Fix: change all three occurrences to "21 機構聯合、22 種機器人" (or similar) to disambiguate. — confidence: high

- **[MED]** 22-multimodal-llm-robotics.md:339 — "**即使是 55B 參數的 RT-2，在 unseen 場景仍有 25% 失敗率**". The DeepMind RT-2 blog + paper report unseen-scenario success rate **62%**, which means the failure rate is **~38%**, not 25%. The 25% number does not match the published evaluation. It may come from a specific sub-slice but is not the headline unseen-scene number. — Fix: either drop the specific number ("unseen 場景仍有相當比例的失敗") or replace with "38% 失敗率（RT-2 unseen scene 62% 成功）". — confidence: high

- **[LOW]** 22-multimodal-llm-robotics.md:121 — "**PaLM-E 12B、PaLI-X 5B、PaLI-X 55B**" in the RT-2 scaling comparison. These numbers are correct when interpreted as RT-2-PaLM-E (12B: 8B LLM + 4B ViT) and RT-2-PaLI-X (5B/55B) variants, but the naming is ambiguous — a careless reader might confuse "PaLM-E 12B" with the main PaLM-E 562B model referenced at :437. Both are correct in their context, but the jump from 12B (RT-2 variant) to 562B (base PaLM-E paper) without explanation will look inconsistent. — Fix: rename to "RT-2-PaLM-E 12B、RT-2-PaLI-X 5B / 55B" at :121 to lock the RT-2 variant context. — confidence: medium

- **[LOW]** 22-multimodal-llm-robotics.md:436 — OXE year "(2024)" is fine (ICRA 2024 proceedings); arXiv is Oct 2023. Not an error but worth standardizing with other citation years in the chapter. — confidence: low
