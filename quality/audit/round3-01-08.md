## Ch03 — C++ Memory Model & Atomics

### New
- **[LOW]** `03-cpp-memory-model-atomics.md:59` — "`std::atomic<T>` 透過硬體指令（`lock cmpxchg` on x86、`ldxr/stxr` on ARM）實現原子性". This was flagged in round-2 as LOW and left unfixed. The sentence implies every atomic op uses CAS/LL-SC primitives, which is wrong for plain atomic load/store on naturally-aligned scalars (these compile to plain `MOV`/`LDR`/`STR`). Only RMW and CAS use `lock cmpxchg` (x86) or `LDXR/STXR` (ARM). Fix: reword to "atomic 的原子性來自硬體保證的自然對齊 load/store（普通 `MOV` / `LDR`/`STR`）；read-modify-write（`fetch_add`、`exchange`、CAS）則需要 `lock`-prefix 指令（x86）或 LL/SC 迴圈（`LDXR`/`STXR`，ARM）". Confidence: HIGH on the textual inaccuracy; LOW on severity because most readers already mentally map it to "used when needed".
